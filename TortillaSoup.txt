Tortilla Soup
2 cans of chicken (kirkland 12.5 oz)
2 jars TJ salsa verde
can of corn (TJ whole kernel) - drain
can of Bush's black beans - drain and rinse
fresh green beans
carrots 
2 small zucchini
1/2 to 1 tsp cayenne pepper
quart TJ low-sodium chicken broth
shredded triple cheese

Chop veggies: Carrots and green beans: about 2/3 corelle bowl of each.
Microwave a little so they are crisp-cooked. Mix all together except
cheese and simmer for a little while to blend flavors. Add cheese on
top in bowl when serving. Serve with tostitos or other corn chips.
